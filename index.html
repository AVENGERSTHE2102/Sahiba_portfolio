ABOUT ME

I am an actor, content creator, and marketing professional with a background in corporate brand management. My journey spans from acting in compelling stories like Laila Majnu, Bard of Blood, and Amar Singh Chamkila, to leading digital strategies at Zomato and Unacademy.

In addition to acting, I serve as a sports presenter for Star Sports and have hosted Shark Tank India. Beyond my professional life, I am deeply passionate about storytelling, lifestyle content, travel, and animal welfare.

Whether I'm on screen, conceptualizing a brand campaign, or exploring new destinations, my primary goal is to tell authentic stories that resonate and connect.
